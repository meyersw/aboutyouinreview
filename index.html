About Me

Greetings,

I am William Meyers and I'm happy to visit with you and tell you a little about myself.

For the past 9 years I have served as the Mathematics instructor for OTC Middle College. This has been the most professionaly and personally fulfulling job I could have hoped for. I often say of my job that I don't have to get up to go to work each day, instead I get to go to work each day.

In addtion to my day job, I have the privlege of working with the Speckman Tutoring and Learning Center here on the OTC campus as well. This is a fantastic resouce for students to use when they need need help in just about any subject of study, or just a quite place to hang out.

In addtion to my professional life, I'm also a father of two great kids, and a grandfather for a very energetic and sassy three year old. Completing my family would be my beautiful wife, Jennifer and our two cats, Jack and Bruiser.
